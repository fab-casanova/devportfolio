Projects

Toxxic

Toxxic is a social media/competition website I created with two other members as the final project for our Cloud Computing course. Toxxic is a site where users can make posts and comments, but there is a twist: all posts must center around another individual, and posters must make a case for why the subject of the post must be banned. At the end of an arbitrary time period, the subject of the most-liked post is banned.

Tools: Python, Microsoft Azure, Cosmos DB, Flask

OCaml Monopoly

I worked with three other team members to create a text-based Monopoly game in OCaml as the final project of our Functional Programming course. Players have full functionality of the game; they move positions, can buy/sell, mortgage properties, and build houses/hotels. It is a text-based implementation that is played on the command terminal, and users can play against other real players or against a random AI player.

Tools: OCaml
